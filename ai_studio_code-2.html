Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to clear up standard questions regarding our trade services.

Are your tradesmen fully insured?

Yes. A M Services carries comprehensive public liability insurance. Every domestic electrical, plumbing, and physical property installation conforms to standard UK regulations.

What areas do you cover beyond Prestonpans?

While we are based in Prestonpans, we service all of East Lothian, Midlothian, and major parts of the city of Edinburgh. Contact us directly if you wish to confirm coverage for your specific postcode.

Do you charge for visual estimates or site surveys?

No, we do not. We offer free, no-obligation quotes for all local electrical, plumbing, bathroom, and kitchen installations.

Can you supply EICR and PAT testing for landlords?

Absolutely. We conduct full electrical inspection and testing (EICR) and Portable Appliance Testing (PAT) for property agencies and individual landlords to satisfy compliance laws.
